What are the Different Types of Real Cash Rummy Games?

Rummy continues to be one of the most popular games amongst Indian players because it has so many variants. You can pick and choose the type of rummy you want to play based on your own personal preferences. Here are some of the most popular variants of rummy.

Points Rummy

The game of Points Rummy can be played even when one doesn’t have a lot of time. The game is played for points (hence the name). The first player who sorts their cards to make sure they have 0 points and makes a valid declaration ends up winning the game.

Pool Rummy

RummyTime offers three sub-variants of Pool Rummy. They are called: 61 Pool, 101 Pool, and 201 Pool. There is a fixed Buy-In to play the game. This becomes part of the prize pool. Players are usually dealt with 13 cards, in case of 13 card rummy, at the start of the game.

The first player to make a valid declaration, which falls under all rummy rules, during each deal will be the winner of that round. They will receive 0 points. Those who lose a particular round will carry the number of points they accumulated to the next round. Players get eliminated if they breach the limit. So, if one is playing 201 Pool rummy, then players who get 201 points or above will be automatically eliminated.

Deals Rummy

What sets Deals Rummy apart from other variants is that at the very beginning of the game, all players are allocated a set number of chips. Players will have to play with these chips for a predetermined number of deals. At the end of every deal, the player who wins (the first player to make a valid declaration) will get all the chips based on the ungrouped cards of opponents in each deal. The player with the most number of chips at the end of all the pre-decided deals will then be declared the winner.
