Rummy players playing real cash games online from Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam and Odisha State are prohibited to play cash games. The state government has passed an Ordinance amending the Gaming Act 1974 banning online Rummy in these states.

However, you can continue to play practice Rummy games for free on our app or website.
